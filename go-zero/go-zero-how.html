How can we reduce our impact?

Simple Swaps

Starting on your zero-waste journey is surprisingly easy, no matter how far along you may be. It starts with the small things; convenience items you might use every day. Below you will find a collection of items that are easily replaceable with more sustainable options, like plastic cutlery for metal, or reusable grocery bags in place of disposable ones. A good rule of thumb is, “If it doesn’t have to be single-use, it probably shouldn’t be.”
